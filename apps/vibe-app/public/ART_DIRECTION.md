# نخبة — Product Image Art Direction Policy

## Framing
- All hero and product images must use **1:1 aspect ratio** (square crop).
- No portrait or landscape crops on the product grid or trending section.

## Background
- Allowed range: `#F2EFE9` → `#FFFFFF` (warm white to pure white).
- No coloured backgrounds, no gradients, no lifestyle context in primary grid images.
- Banner / editorial hero images may use lifestyle photography with gradient overlay.

## Light source
- All product shadows cast from **upper-right at 20°** (consistent across catalog).
- Shadow colour: warm neutral, never pure black. Use `rgba(14, 12, 9, 0.12)`.

## Subject clearance
- Product must occupy 70–85% of frame height.
- Minimum 8% negative space on all four sides.

## Compression
- WebP, quality 85, max 400 KB for grid cards.
- WebP, quality 88, max 900 KB for banner/hero slots.

## File naming
`{product-id}_{color-slug}_{view}.webp`  
Example: `1042_black_front.webp`
